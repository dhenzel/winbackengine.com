Zenoti is one of the most powerful platforms in the beauty and wellness space. It handles booking, POS, inventory, marketing, and client management for thousands of salons, spas, and med spas worldwide.


But here’s what Zenoti can’t do on its own: pick up the phone and call a client who stopped coming in.


If you’re a multi-location operator running on Zenoti, your dashboard is full of lapsed clients. People who loved your service, booked multiple times, then quietly disappeared. Zenoti tracked every visit, every dollar they spent, every service they booked. That data is gold. But without action, it’s just a database entry.


## Why Zenoti Users Still Lose Clients


Zenoti gives you tools for automated campaigns, email marketing, and even two-way texting. So why do most operators still see 60%+ annual client churn?


**Automation has a ceiling.** Zenoti’s marketing automation is solid for appointment reminders and promotional blasts. But reactivating a lapsed client is fundamentally different from reminding an active one about their next appointment. Lapsed clients have disengaged. They’re ignoring your emails.


**Segmentation alone doesn’t convert.** You can build the most sophisticated segment in Zenoti’s Smart Marketing, filter by last visit date, lifetime value, service type. But if the output is another email that gets buried in their inbox, the segmentation didn’t help.


**The personal touch is missing.** Zenoti was built to scale operations, not to have one-on-one conversations with churned clients. That’s not a knock on the platform. It’s just not what it’s designed for.


## The Zenoti Data Advantage


Where Zenoti does shine is in the quality of data it gives you for reactivation campaigns. Here’s what you can pull:


- **Last visit date and service type.** Know exactly when they stopped and what they were getting.

- **Lifetime spend.** Prioritize high-value clients first.

- **Membership status.** Flag cancelled memberships for targeted win-back offers.

- **Preferred stylist or therapist.** This matters. “Hi Lisa, Amanda wanted me to reach out because she noticed you haven’t been in for a while” hits different than a generic message.

- **Visit frequency patterns.** Someone who came weekly vs. monthly needs a different approach.


When you feed this data into a dedicated reactivation campaign with real human agents making calls, the results are dramatically different from what automated tools deliver.


## How Phone Outreach Works with Zenoti


**Export and segment.** Pull your lapsed client list from Zenoti, filtered by whatever criteria matter to your business. Most operators start with “hasn’t visited in 3+ weeks” and sort by lifetime value.


**Brief the agents.** The call isn’t a cold pitch. It’s a personalized check-in. The agent knows the client’s name, their usual service, their preferred provider, and when they last visited. The conversation feels like it’s coming from the salon, not a call center.


**Track attribution.** After the calls, monitor Zenoti for rebookings within a 7-30 day window. Cross-reference dialed clients with new bookings to measure the direct impact.


## Real Numbers from Multi-Location Operators


Operators using Zenoti who add dedicated phone reactivation typically see:


- **25-40% of called clients rebook** within 30 days

- **$80-200 per reactivated client** in immediate revenue

- **3x+ ROI** on the cost of the outreach program


For a 10-location operation with 500 lapsed clients per location, even conservative reactivation rates translate to hundreds of thousands in recovered annual revenue.


## Zenoti + Phone Outreach: The Playbook


**Week 1-2:** Export lapsed client data, set up call campaigns, brief agents on your brand and services.


**Week 3-4:** Start calling. Prioritize high-LTV clients and recently lapsed (3-6 week window).


**Month 2+:** Expand to birthday campaigns, referral outreach, and seasonal re-engagement. Use Zenoti’s data to time everything perfectly.


**Ongoing:** Review call recordings, track rebooking rates, adjust scripts based on what’s converting.


## The Bottom Line for Zenoti Users


You chose Zenoti because you wanted a platform that could run your operations at scale. It does that well. But the operational data sitting in your system is also your biggest untapped revenue source.


What I love about working with Zenoti-based businesses is the speed of impact. You’re not waiting months to see if something works. Within the first week, you see rebookings coming in from clients you’d written off. It’s real revenue, recovered fast, from people who already know and trust your brand.


Automated campaigns will always have their place. But for the high-value, high-touch work of winning back lapsed clients, nothing beats a real conversation. Your Zenoti data makes those conversations targeted and personal. That combination is where the real results happen.
